# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

Welcome! This repository centralizes all project management knowledge for OctoAcme. Use this README as your starting point for understanding processes, roles, and best practices.

## Summary of OctoAcme Project Management Processes

### Initiation
Craft a one-pager to define problems, goals, stakeholders, and success metrics. Gain alignment and approval before planning.

### Planning
Develop a prioritized backlog, set milestones, estimate work, and identify risks and dependencies. Define criteria for success and done.

### Execution & Tracking
Use project boards, regular standups, and demos to track progress and resolve blockers. Follow branching and CI/CD conventions for quality.

### Risk Management & Communication
Maintain a risk register, review risks at syncs, and use clear weekly updates and escalation paths for stakeholders.

### Release & Deployment
Standardized checklists ensure releases are predictable, tested, and communicated. Rollback plans and incident protocols are documented.

### Retrospectives & Continuous Improvement
Conduct retros after major releases or milestones, record lessons learned, and track action items for team improvements.

### Roles & Responsibilities
Clear descriptions for PM, PdM, Developer, QA, and Stakeholder roles are documented for accountability and onboarding.
